TechFlow Pricing & Plans
========================

Choose the plan that fits your team's needs.

FREE PLAN - $0/month
--------------------
Perfect for individuals and small teams getting started.

Includes:
- Up to 5 team members
- 3 projects
- Basic task management
- Board and List views
- 100 MB file storage
- Mobile app access
- Email support (48-hour response)

Limitations:
- No timeline or calendar views
- No custom fields
- No automations
- No integrations
- TechFlow branding on shared views

PRO PLAN - $12/user/month (billed annually) or $15/month-to-month
-----------------------------------------------------------------
For growing teams that need more power and flexibility.

Everything in Free, plus:
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited projects
- Timeline and Calendar views
- Custom fields (text, numbers, dropdowns)
- 10 GB file storage per user
- Basic automations (up to 50/month)
- Slack and Google Drive integrations
- Priority email support (24-hour response)
- Remove TechFlow branding

Best for: Teams of 5-50 people

BUSINESS PLAN - $24/user/month (billed annually) or $30/month-to-month
----------------------------------------------------------------------
For organizations that need advanced features and security.

Everything in Pro, plus:
- Advanced automations (unlimited)
- All integrations (GitHub, Zoom, Figma, etc.)
- Custom workflows and templates
- Advanced reporting and analytics
- 50 GB file storage per user
- Admin controls and permissions
- SSO (Single Sign-On) with SAML
- Live chat support (4-hour response)
- Audit logs

Best for: Teams of 50-500 people

ENTERPRISE PLAN - Custom pricing
--------------------------------
For large organizations with complex needs.

Everything in Business, plus:

Contact sales@techflow.io for a custom quote.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
--------------------------

Q: Can I try Pro or Business before buying?
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of any paid plan. No credit card required.
   Go to Settings > Billing > Start Trial.

Q: What happens when my trial ends?
A: Your workspace automatically moves to the Free plan. No data is lost, but
   you'll lose access to premium features until you upgrade.

Q: Can I change plans anytime?
A: Yes. Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades take effect at the
   end of your billing cycle.

Q: Do you offer discounts?
A: Yes! We offer:
   - 20% off annual billing (vs monthly)
   - 50% off for nonprofits and educational institutions
   - 25% off for startups (under 2 years old, under $1M funding)
   Contact sales@techflow.io for discount codes.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal.
   Enterprise customers can pay by invoice with NET-30 terms.

Q: Is there a refund policy?
A: Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan,
   contact support@techflow.io for a full refund.
