TechFlow Troubleshooting Guide
==============================

Common issues and how to resolve them.

ACCOUNT & LOGIN ISSUES
----------------------

"I forgot my password"
Solution:
1. Go to app.techflow.io/login
2. Click "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for reset link (check spam folder too)
5. Click the link and create a new password
Note: Reset links expire after 24 hours.

"I'm not receiving the password reset email"
Solutions:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@techflow.io to your contacts
- Try a different email if you have multiple accounts
- Contact support@techflow.io if still not working

"My account is locked"
This happens after 5 failed login attempts.
Solution:
- Wait 30 minutes, then try again
- Or click "Forgot password?" to reset immediately
- Contact support if you suspect unauthorized access

"I can't access my workspace"
Possible causes:
- Your account was removed by an admin
- The workspace was deleted
- Your email domain changed
Solution: Contact your workspace admin or support@techflow.io

TASK & PROJECT ISSUES
---------------------

"I can't create new tasks"
Possible causes:
- You don't have permission (Guest users can't create tasks)
- The project is archived
- Free plan limit reached (3 projects max)
Solutions:
- Ask your admin to change your role to Member
- Unarchive the project (Projects > Archived > Restore)
- Upgrade to Pro plan for unlimited projects

"Tasks are not saving"
Possible causes:
- Network connection issue
- Browser cache problem
- Session expired
Solutions:
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Log out and log back in

"I accidentally deleted a task"
Solution:
1. Go to the project
2. Click the "..." menu > "View deleted items"
3. Find the task and click "Restore"
Note: Deleted items are kept for 30 days.

"My task due dates are wrong"
This is usually a timezone issue.
Solution:
1. Go to Settings > Profile
2. Check your timezone setting
3. Update to your correct timezone
4. Refresh the page

NOTIFICATION ISSUES
-------------------

"I'm not getting email notifications"
Solutions:
1. Check Settings > Notifications > Email
2. Make sure email notifications are enabled
3. Check if specific notification types are turned off
4. Add notifications@techflow.io to your contacts
5. Check your spam folder

"I'm getting too many notifications"
Solutions:
1. Go to Settings > Notifications
2. Disable notifications you don't need:
   - Uncheck "All activity on tasks I follow"
   - Uncheck "Daily digest emails"
3. Use "Mute" on specific projects or tasks
4. Set "Do Not Disturb" hours

"Slack notifications aren't working"
Solutions:
1. Check if the Slack integration is connected:
   - Workspace Settings > Integrations > Slack
2. Make sure the TechFlow bot is in the channel
3. Reinstall the integration if needed
4. Check Slack's own notification settings

SYNC & PERFORMANCE ISSUES
-------------------------

"The app is slow"
Solutions:
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Close other tabs to free memory
- Check your internet speed

"Changes aren't syncing between devices"
Solutions:
- Make sure you're online on both devices
- Pull down to refresh on mobile
- Press F5 or Ctrl+R on desktop
- Log out and log back in
- Check for app updates (mobile)

"The mobile app keeps crashing"
Solutions:
- Update to the latest app version
- Restart your phone
- Clear the app cache:
  - iOS: Delete and reinstall the app
  - Android: Settings > Apps > TechFlow > Clear Cache
- Make sure you have enough storage space

FILE & ATTACHMENT ISSUES
------------------------

"I can't upload files"
Possible causes:
- File is too large (max 100MB per file)
- Storage limit reached
- File type not supported
Solutions:
- Compress large files before uploading
- Upgrade plan for more storage
- Supported types: Images, PDFs, Office docs, text files

"Attachments aren't loading"
Solutions:
- Check your internet connection
- Try opening in a new tab
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

"I accidentally deleted an attachment"
Unfortunately, deleted attachments cannot be recovered.
Recommendation: Keep important files backed up elsewhere.

BILLING ISSUES
--------------

"I was charged incorrectly"
Contact support@techflow.io with:
- Your workspace name
- The charge amount and date
- Screenshot of the charge
We'll investigate within 24 hours.

"My card was declined"
Possible causes:
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Bank blocked the transaction
Solutions:
- Try a different card
- Contact your bank to authorize the charge
- Use PayPal as an alternative

"I need a refund"
Policy: Full refund within 30 days of purchase.
Contact support@techflow.io with your workspace name.

"I need an invoice"
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Billing History"
3. Click "Download" next to any payment
Invoices are also emailed after each payment.

STILL NEED HELP?
----------------

If your issue isn't listed here:

1. Search our Help Center: help.techflow.io
2. Ask the Community: community.techflow.io
3. Email Support: support@techflow.io
4. Live Chat: Click the chat icon (Pro plan and above)

When contacting support, please include:
- Your email address
- Workspace name
- Description of the issue
- Screenshots if applicable
- Browser/device information
